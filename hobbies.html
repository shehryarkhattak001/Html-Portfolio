My Favorite Games

Online Game: PUBG Mobile

PUBG Mobile is my favorite online game. It is a battle royale game where 100 players parachute onto an island and fight to be the last one standing. I love the strategy, teamwork, and excitement it provides. I usually play with my friends in squad mode.

Physical Game: Cricket

Cricket is my favorite physical sport. I enjoy playing it with friends on weekends. I am a good bowler and love the team spirit and competition that comes with the game. Playing cricket keeps me fit and active.
